About Me

I am a soon-to-be PhD graduate specializing in the development of advanced electromechanical systems. My expertise lies in actuator and generator design, controller development, and embedded systems. I am passionate about creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between mechanical and electronic systems.

For more information, please visit my Resume.

Research Interest

  • Advanced Motor Design: Expertise in the design and optimization of BLDC motors and electromagnetic braking systems for precision applications.
  • Energy Conversion Systems: Development of custom generators for specialized uses, including fuzes, wind turbines, and other tailored energy generation solutions.
  • Control System Development: Design and implementation of linear and non-linear controllers for high-performance motor control and electromechanical systems.
  • Embedded Systems: Firmware development for motor control applications, integrating advanced algorithms (FOC) and system-level optimization.

Research Highlights

  • Multiphysics Analysis of a High Speed Eddy Current Brake.
  • Electromagnetic Torque Control of an Optimized Flux-Concentrated Transverse Flux Generator.
  • Robust Velocity Control of a Fixed Canard Decoupled Dual-Spin Projectile.
  • Design of Radial Flux Outrunner BLDC Motor for Single-Rotor UAVs.
  • Electromagnetic Refinement and Performance Characterization of a UAV BLDC Motor.
